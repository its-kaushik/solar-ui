If you're researching rooftop solar for your home in Delhi, the first thing you want to know is: **how much will it cost?** Let's break it down for the most popular system size — 3 KW — which is ideal for most 2-3 BHK homes.

## Total System Cost (Before Subsidy)

A 3 KW on-grid rooftop solar system in Delhi typically costs between **Rs 1,80,000 to Rs 2,20,000** including installation. This covers:

| Component | Approximate Cost |
|---|---|
| Solar panels (3 KW) | Rs 75,000 – 90,000 |
| Inverter (string/micro) | Rs 30,000 – 45,000 |
| Mounting structure | Rs 15,000 – 20,000 |
| Wiring, earthing, SPD, lightning arrestor | Rs 15,000 – 20,000 |
| Installation & labour | Rs 15,000 – 20,000 |
| Net meter & DISCOM coordination | Rs 10,000 – 15,000 |
| **Total** | **Rs 1,80,000 – 2,20,000** |

Prices vary based on panel brand (Adani, Vikram, etc.), inverter type, and roof complexity.

## Government Subsidies You Can Claim

Delhi residents get two subsidies that dramatically reduce the cost:

### 1. PM Surya Ghar Yojana (Central Government)

For a 3 KW system, you get **Rs 78,000** in subsidy:

- First 2 KW: Rs 30,000/KW = Rs 60,000
- Next 1 KW: Rs 18,000/KW = Rs 18,000
- **Total: Rs 78,000** (disbursed via DBT to your bank account)

### 2. Delhi State Subsidy

The Delhi government provides an additional **Rs 2,000 per KW** plus a **Rs 30,000 bonus for 3 KW systems**:

- 3 KW × Rs 2,000 = Rs 6,000
- Bonus for 3 KW: Rs 30,000
- **Total: Up to Rs 30,000** (disbursed after commissioning)

### Combined Subsidy

| Subsidy | Amount |
|---|---|
| PM Surya Ghar (Central) | Rs 78,000 |
| Delhi State Subsidy | Up to Rs 30,000 |
| **Total Subsidy** | **Up to Rs 1,08,000** |

## Your Net Cost After Subsidy

| | Amount |
|---|---|
| System cost | Rs 1,80,000 – 2,20,000 |
| Minus total subsidy | Rs 1,08,000 |
| **Your net cost** | **Rs 70,000 – 1,10,000** |

That's your total out-of-pocket cost for a system that will generate free electricity for 25+ years.

## What About EMI / Financing?

If you don't want to pay the full amount upfront, solar loans are available from several banks. Typical terms:

- **Loan amount:** Rs 1.5 – 2 lakh (pre-subsidy)
- **Interest rate:** 7 – 9% per annum
- **Tenure:** 3 – 5 years
- **EMI:** Roughly Rs 3,500 – 4,500/month for a 5-year loan

In most cases, **your EMI will be less than your current electricity bill**, so you start saving from month one.

## What's the Payback Period?

With a 3 KW system generating approximately 360 units/month:

- **Monthly electricity savings:** 360 × Rs 7 = Rs 2,520
- **Monthly GBI income:** 360 × Rs 3 = Rs 1,080
- **Total monthly benefit:** Rs 3,600
- **Annual benefit:** Rs 43,200

At a net cost of Rs 70,000 – 1,10,000, your **payback period is under 3 years**. After that, it's all profit for the remaining 22+ years of panel life.
